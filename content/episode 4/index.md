<div class="content is-small">
What does it mean to be “Masculine of center” when you were born in a “female” body? Though now great strides are being made to not only “think” outside the binary, but LIVE outside the binary, its can still be complicated to navigate the messy spaces of beliefs in the binary have created. From unlearning “toxic masculinity” to finding strength in “femininity” things are being bounced around and turned upside down. I want to talk a little about that that means and how we ALL navigate it.

<b> <a href="https://www.goingwiththefro.com/" target="blank">AFRONAUTIC </a> aka Liselle (Lee-sell)</b> was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. A self taught photographer and content creator, she travels, blogs and creates content for small brands centered around QPOC.

<br>
<br>
<b> Jeaux</b> is a masc-identified, non-binary boi that is passionate about creativity, food and bringing queer bros together, throughout the diaspora. Jeaux is a DJ, an amazing cook, loves to dance and makes music in order to free themselves. In the midst of navigating this planet they’ve created a virtual safe space called <a href="https://www.blackboicomplex.com/" target="blank">BlackBoiComplex.</a> This is a platform honoring, highlighting and empowering BlackQTLGBAI+ masculine identified people through Black Boi Complex, Jeaux helps connect people across the world, providing clarity, unity and strength to those who need it most.
